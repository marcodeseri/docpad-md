--- layout: post title: fastPath tags: [] status: publish category: hp-highlights published: true --- fastPath is a chrome extension that aufofills forms for you and helps you checkout faster and safer. fastPath makes your shopping experience much better, securely storing your favorite addresses and making this data available when you need it. fastPath pre-fills forms for you and conveniently provides you access to your data from an elegant popover layer when you encounter a web form to fill. It is fast, free and secure: data are exchanged over a SSL connection and never stored to your computer.
