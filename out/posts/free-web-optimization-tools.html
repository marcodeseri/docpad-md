Free Web Optimization Tools

On Sun Oct 02 2011 By marco
Enterprise web optimization tools are expensive. They provide incredible features, but are often heavy on your budget. Before taking the leap and investing in those products, start by getting acquainted to higher conversion rates using free tools. Today, we'll be taking a first look at two sets of products:
  • Web Performance Optimization
  • Content Targeting

Web Performance Optimization with Cloudflare

Cloudflare offers a unique opportunity to small site owners: a free cdn that speeds up the loading of static assets. The company also puts a lot of emphasis in the security features they offer, filtering out malicious traffic that comes to your site.

All you have to do to start using the service is sign up and modify your dns settings so they resolve to cloudflare. Traffic to your domain will be routed and processed by cloudflare: they will be caching static assets (images, css and js files) and serve them from servers geographically closer to your users. The added benefit is a reduced load on your servers, that will have to respond to a lower number of requests. There are several configurations available: you can decide the kind of caching style to use (basic or aggressive) and if you want to minify and compress static resources. They are also adding up a growing number of optimization services, to secure and lighten your site impact on your servers.

Content Targeting

BT Buckets is a simple content targeting tool, integrated wih Google Analytics. Browser extensions for Chrome and Firefox will help you identify your audience, exposing a BT Bucket icon on GA web interface (old interface for now). You can also leverage pre-defined segments that come from BT Buckets. You can then manipulate the DOM in many ways for each of your target groups and measure how the changes improve your site performance.
